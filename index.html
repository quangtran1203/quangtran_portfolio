About Me

My name is Quang Tran and I am a third year student majoring in Computer Science at Lassonde School of Engineering – York University. I am an international student from Vietnam who set foot in Canada at the age of 17.

I have always been interested in Mathematics and Open Science but learning just raw theories may not be something that I enjoy. As a result, I went with something more hands-on in which I can actually put theories into practice – Computer Science. My background in Mathematics and Design System has proven to be valuable in the field of Software Engineering.
I always seek to explore new concepts, take risks, brainstorm and solve complex puzzles. Being born and raised in an open-minded and diligent family, I carry high hopes that my parents have on me. I was never refrained from learning, so I have been exposed to a lot of real-world problems. All that is left for me to do is to accumulate knowledge and experience to look for solutions to these problems.
A diligent university student and an enthusiastic Software / Front-End Developer with basic understanding of Back-End process. Excellent adaptability and attention to detail. Keen on solving Math and algorithmic problems. Always eager to broaden my horizons and explore new technologies. Always strive for productivity and efficiency in building scalable applications. Possess diverse skillset - thrive under pressure and in an agile, fast-paced environment.
